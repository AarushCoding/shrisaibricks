THE PROCESS

A high-contrast look at our manufacturing standards.

01

Raw Material Mixing

Industrial-grade fly ash and lime are mathematically proportioned and mixed in high-torque pan mixers for structural consistency.

02

High-Pressure Molding

Using 100+ tons of hydraulic force, the mixture is compressed into precise dimensions, ensuring zero internal voids.

03

Controlled Curing

Bricks undergo a 21-day steam or moisture curing cycle to trigger the chemical bonding required for high load-bearing capacity.

04

Quality Testing

Every batch is tested for compressive strength and water absorption to meet ISO and local building standards.
