Durability

Fly ash bricks (lightweight) possess high compressive strength (90-100 kg/cm²), making them less prone to breakage during transport and superior for load-bearing compared to moderate-strength red bricks.

Light Weight

Lower density reduces the overall dead weight of structures, making them ideal for high-rise buildings and decreasing seismic load.

Eco-Friendly

Manufactured using fly ash — industrial waste from power plants — these bricks help reduce environmental pollution.
